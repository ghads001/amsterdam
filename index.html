Discover Amsterdam's Secret Treasures

Venture beyond the typical tourist spots and explore these lesser-known gems of Amsterdam. Each location offers a unique glimpse into the city's rich culture and history.

Micropia

The world's first museum of microbes, offering a fascinating look at the invisible life all around us.

Website: https://www.micropia.nl/en/

Opening Times: Daily 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Quiet Times: Weekday mornings, especially Tuesday and Wednesday

Transport: Tram 14 to Artis, or Metro 51, 53, 54 to Waterlooplein

Oosterpark

A beautiful park in the eastern part of Amsterdam, known for its diverse bird population and cultural monuments.

Website: https://www.amsterdam.nl/en/parks/oosterpark/

Opening Times: Open 24/7

Quiet Times: Early mornings and weekdays

Transport: Tram 3 or 7 to Eerste van Swindenstraat

Museum Ons' Lieve Heer op Solder

A 17th-century canal house with a hidden Catholic church in its attic, offering a glimpse into religious persecution history.

Website: https://www.opsolder.nl/en

Opening Times: Monday to Saturday 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Sunday 1:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Quiet Times: Weekday mornings, especially Tuesday and Wednesday

Transport: Metro 51, 53, 54 to Nieuwmarkt, or walk from Central Station

Hortus Botanicus

One of the oldest botanic gardens in the world, featuring rare plants and beautiful greenhouses.

Website: https://www.dehortus.nl/en/

Opening Times: Daily 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Quiet Times: Weekday afternoons, especially Monday and Tuesday

Transport: Tram 14 to Artis, or Metro 51, 53, 54 to Waterlooplein

Rembrandt House Museum

The restored house where Rembrandt lived and worked, offering insights into the artist's life and techniques.

Website: https://www.rembrandthuis.nl/en/

Opening Times: Daily 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Quiet Times: Early mornings on weekdays, especially Tuesday and Wednesday

Transport: Metro 51, 53, 54 to Waterlooplein, or Tram 14 to Waterlooplein

NDSM Wharf

A former shipyard turned creative hub, featuring street art, cultural events, and unique dining experiences.

Website: https://www.ndsm.nl/en/

Opening Times: Area accessible 24/7, individual venue times vary

Quiet Times: Weekday mornings and early afternoons

Transport: Free ferry from Amsterdam Central Station (runs 24/7)

Tropenmuseum

An ethnographic museum housed in a stunning building, exploring human culture worldwide.

Website: https://www.tropenmuseum.nl/en

Opening Times: Tuesday to Sunday 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Quiet Times: Tuesday and Wednesday mornings

Transport: Tram 14 to Tropenmuseum, or Bus 22 to Eerste van Swindenstraat

Foam

A photography museum showcasing both established and emerging talent in a beautiful canal house setting.

Website: https://www.foam.org/home

Opening Times: Daily 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Thursday and Friday until 9:00 PM

Quiet Times: Weekday mornings, especially Monday and Tuesday

Transport: Tram 16, 24 to Keizersgracht

Oude Kerk

Amsterdam's oldest building and parish church, now a cultural center hosting art installations and events.

Website: https://oudekerk.nl/en/

Opening Times: Monday to Saturday 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Sunday 1:00 PM - 5:30 PM

Quiet Times: Early mornings on weekdays, especially Tuesday and Wednesday

Transport: Metro 51, 53, 54 to Nieuwmarkt, or walk from Central Station

Eyk Sloot

A hidden canal offering a peaceful escape from the bustling city, perfect for a quiet stroll.

Website: N/A (Public space)

Opening Times: Accessible 24/7

Quiet Times: Early mornings and weekday afternoons

Transport: Tram 4 to Frederiksplein, then a short walk

Begijnhof

A hidden courtyard dating back to the Middle Ages, featuring historic houses and a serene atmosphere.

Website: https://www.begijnhofamsterdam.nl/en/

Opening Times: Daily 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Quiet Times: Early mornings, especially on weekdays

Transport: Tram 1, 2, 5 to Spui, or walk from Dam Square

Noordermarkt

A charming square hosting various markets throughout the week, including an organic farmers' market on Saturdays.

Website: https://www.iamsterdam.com/en/see-and-do/shopping/overview-markets/noordermarkt

Opening Times: Monday 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM (Flea Market), Saturday 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM (Farmers' Market)

Quiet Times: Early mornings just as the markets open

Transport: Bus 18 to Noorderkerk, or Tram 5 to Westermarkt

De Poezenboot (The Cat Boat)

A unique animal sanctuary - a floating barge home to dozens of cats on one of Amsterdam's canals.

Website: https://depoezenboot.nl/en/

Opening Times: Tuesday to Sunday, 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM

Quiet Times: Weekday afternoons, especially Tuesday and Wednesday

Transport: Walk from Central Station, or take tram 13, 17 to Westermarkt
